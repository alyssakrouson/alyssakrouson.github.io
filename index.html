Welcome!

My name is Alyssa Krouson. I am a graduate student from the University of Rhode Island where I studied computer science and Japanese. Here you can find information about me, my vairous projects I made during my studies, as well as my studies in language and linguistics.

I grew up in Westwood Massachusetts and graduated from Westwood High School in 2020. The following four years, I stayed in Kingston, Rhode Island, and went to the University of Rhode Island.

After starting my computer science studies, I learned JavaScript, Python, C++, HTML, and CSS (which I used to create this website).

I began learning Japanese in 2022 and progressed to an intermediate proficiency level. In the summer of 2023, I studied abroad in South Korea, where I studied Korean. I also studied French for five years in middle school and high school, bringing me to an intermediate high/advanced low proficiency level.

I hope to continue working in web design as well as traveling and learning languages in the years to come.
